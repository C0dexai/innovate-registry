Core Concept: Operator-Centric Augmentation

The entire ecosystem is designed as an intelligent co-pilot for the human operator. The AI Family agents, supervised by LYRA, automate complex tasks, provide contextual insights, and manage the development lifecycle, allowing the operator to focus on high-level strategy and decision-making.

Standardized Application APIs

To ensure seamless integration, all 15 applications expose a consistent set of RESTful endpoints. This allows the orchestrator to submit tasks, monitor progress, and retrieve results in a uniform way. Click each endpoint to see details.

Automated CI/CD Orchestration

The platform automates its own development lifecycle. A single `git push` triggers a sophisticated, multi-agent workflow that handles everything from semantic analysis to deployment, ensuring consistency and reliability across the ecosystem.
